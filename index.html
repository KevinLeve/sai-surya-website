Who We Are

Sai Surya SDN BHD is a professional recycling and waste management company based in Brunei, specializing in scrap metal collection, industrial waste handling, and sustainable recycling solutions.

We are committed to delivering safe, efficient, and environmentally responsible services tailored to the needs of industries and businesses. Our processes are designed to maximize material recovery while minimizing environmental impact.

With a strong operational foundation and a forward-thinking approach, we aim to contribute to a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future.
